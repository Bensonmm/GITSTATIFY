About Us

GitHub Insights Portal was born out of our collective passion for leveraging data analytics to optimize our development workflows. As developers ourselves, we often found it challenging to gain comprehensive insights into our GitHub activity and identify areas for improvement. This project started as a personal endeavor to address this challenge and evolved into a collaborative effort to create a tool that empowers developers to unlock the full potential of their GitHub contributions.

This project is a part of our Portfolio Projects at ALX AFRICA , where we hone our skills and collaborate on real-world projects to showcase our expertise to potential employers.
